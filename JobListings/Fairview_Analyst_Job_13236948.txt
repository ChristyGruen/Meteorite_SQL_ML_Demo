Job Description:	**Overview**

Are you ready for the next step in your data analyst career? M Health Fairview is hiring a Population Health Senior Health Information Data Analyst!

This Sr. Analyst excels in data analysis and reporting related to Population Health, Value Based Care contracts, and Care Coordination. This role includes using and supporting Epic's Healthy Planet tools to provide data and technology to operational staff and leaders to look after patients and provide understanding of our success in Value Based Contracts.They work in a team to craft, develop, and implement data transformation into information that is useful. The Sr. Data Analyst conducts health information analysis as well as the reviews, assesses, validates, and develops with data management processes to support Fairview's health initiatives. This role is key to supporting system and network level clinical and financial performance. The Analyst will ensure innovative quality and population-focused analysis and reporting on quality, risk, utilization, cost and clinical measures through the use of, but not limited to, regulatory specifications, benchmarking, trending, opportunity analysis and predictive modeling. The senior role works independently to craft, document, and develop solutions to deliver data and analytics to clinical and operational senior and executive leaders. Senior team members may also work independently with strategic partners, assuring that any work and data sharing conform with Fairview's standards and align with our legal and regulatory obligations. This team-member will use standard processes in development, testing, and deployment of population health technology, data, and analytics solutions. They will be the primary resource in conducting peer review of team members work product prior to user acceptance testing.

This work from home opportunity is full time (80 hours/2 weeks) andbenefit (https://www.fairview.org/benefits) eligible. We offer medical, dental, and vision coverage along with PTO and 403B.

**Responsibilities**

+ Participates in discussions with teams and leadership to craft analytic reports for quality reporting and population health management using electronic medical record data, claims and other data sources.

+ Accountable and proficient in: defining requirements, data validation, and supervision of report development to support enterprise analytics, statistics, predictive modeling and external data submissions.

+ Work directly with the Health Information Report Writers, Data Modelers and/or ETL Developers to build data driven reporting that is relevant and useful for the Fairview enterprise, and affiliated networks

+ Proficient in analyzing, visualizing, and modeling data to provide insight and practical recommendations.

+ Partner with data scientists, quality teams and researchers to develop and implement algorithms for performing predictive and prescriptive analytics.

+ Proficient in applying appropriate tests to analytical results to deliver high quality and accurate results.

+ Assists in the review process of data sent to external vendors by Fairview. Includes working directly with the external vendors to understand the business rules application to the data extracts.

+ Researches and reviews benchmarking data for comparison, validation and scope. Identifies areas for greatest opportunity for intervention.

+ Builds written narrative summaries that outline project purpose, methodology, summary of findings, trends of interest, and opportunities for consideration.

+ Provides technical, analytical and business mentorship to other team members and other groups with Fairview.

+ Proficient in: Displaying original thinking and creativity; Meets challenges with resourcefulness; Generates suggestions for improving work; Develops innovative ways and ideas; Presents ideas and information in a manner that gets others attention.
